Start a Chapter

Lead the ripple in your own city.

GiveAMemory chapters are led entirely by young people who bring art, comfort, and connection to elders living with dementia and Alzheimer’s — right in their own community.

What’s a Chapter?

A local team, led by you, bringing GiveAMemory home.

Every chapter is youth-led, just like our founding team. As a chapter lead, you’ll partner with a care facility near you, gather volunteers from your school or neighborhood, and organize regular art drop-offs — turning a single afternoon of connection into an ongoing source of comfort for the residents you serve.
