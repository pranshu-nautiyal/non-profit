Meet the Founding Team

Our team is entirely youth-led, reflecting our commitment to putting young people at the center of caregiving and creativity. Together, they oversee our programs, partnerships, and outreach, making sure every art kit and every visit is thoughtful, effective, and full of heart.

Image placeholder

Mithil Shah

CEO & Co-Founder

Mithil co-founded the organization after watching a single piece of art bring his grandmother comfort during her battle with Alzheimer’s. He leads the team’s vision, partnerships, and overall strategy.
