Partner With Us

Partner with GiveAMemory.

Whether you run a care facility, fund good ideas, make art supplies, or lead a school club — partnering with us means reaching more patients, faster.

Why Partner

We’re a youth-led initiative looking for partners who want to grow this with us.

GiveAMemory is still early — youth-run, and not yet a registered nonprofit. That means every partnership, big or small, directly funds art supplies, shipping, and new chapters, instead of disappearing into overhead.

Care Facilities

Host a GiveAMemory chapter at your facility and receive art kits and regular visits from local volunteers.

Funders & Sponsors

Help cover the cost of art supplies, shipping, and starter kits for new chapters.

Art & Supply Companies

Donate materials, shipping services, or offer a partner discount to keep kits flowing.

Schools & Youth Organizations

Turn a one-time club project into an ongoing partnership with a GiveAMemory chapter.

Get in Touch

Ready to partner with us?

Tell us a bit about your organization and what you have in mind — we’ll follow up soon.

Not a partner organization?

You can still make a difference as an individual volunteer, or by supporting us directly.
