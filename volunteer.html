Volunteer to Create

A simple, meaningful way to bring color into someone’s day.

Whether you’re a student, a classroom, a scout troop, or a family looking for a weekend project, creating art for our patients is one of the easiest ways to get involved. No sign-up forms, no ongoing commitment — just a little time, a few art supplies, and a willingness to make someone smile.

Gather Your Supplies

Grab crayons, markers, colored pencils, or paint. Keep it simple — bold colors and big, clear shapes are easiest for our patients to enjoy.

Create Something Cheerful

Draw a picture, make a card, or write a short, warm note. Nature, animals, flowers, and kind words are always a hit.

Send It Our Way

Mail or drop off your artwork and we’ll make sure it reaches someone who needs it. Reach out to givingamemory@gmail.com for mailing and drop-off details.

What Patients Receive

Every piece is different, and that’s the point.

There’s no template to follow. A crayon drawing, a painted card, a handwritten note — what matters is that a real person made it, for someone they may never meet.
